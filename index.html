BRIEF INTRODUCTION

我们生活在一个信息过载的时代，人们无时无刻不在接收着大量被加工解析的信息，某些平台会让你的时间在碎片化的信息里面消磨掉。我们面前无所不有，但当我们陷入焦虑时也许会感到我们一无所有。所以我们很多时候只需要一个短暂的休息时间，来恢复大脑的工作效率。因此我们意在设计一个能够帮助人们冥想休息，放松身心的空间，包括空间中的各种设施等，让体验者能够回到现实世界，放松大脑，提高效率。 
Introduction: We live in an era of information overload. People receive a lot of information that is processed and analyzed all the time. Some platforms will let your time be consumed in fragmented information. There is everything in front of us, but when we are in anxiety, we may feel that we have nothing. So many times we only need a short break to restore the working efficiency of the brain. Therefore, we intend to design a space that can help people meditate to rest and relax, including various facilities in the space, so that the experiencer can return to the real world, relax the brain, and improve efficiency. 

CONNECTION

与选择的幻觉的联系：引导消费者选择我们的产品，并通过营造强烈氛围，给用户带来与信息时代隔绝的幻觉，带领用户更快的进入冥想。
Connection with the illusion of choice: guide consumers to choose our products, and bring the illusion of isolation from the information age to users by creating a strong atmosphere, leading users to enter meditation faster.

冥想空间 Meditation Space

整个装置外形：球形
门的形状：拱形
选择原因：球形和圆弧形对比矩形和多边形来说会减弱边界感，让用户感受更加广阔的空间，在室内也能最大限度地地创造空旷的幻觉。
视觉效果：玻璃的透明外壳给人以纯净通透的感觉。进入后两层玻璃内会有烟雾升起，给人以虚无缥缈、与世隔绝之感，也直观地表现出放空时的状态。
整体体量：直径约为2.5m
选择原因：能够放置于一般的室内空间，也符合人体的边界感
The overall shape of the device: spherical
The shape of the door: arched
Reason for selection: Compared with rectangle and polygon, spherical and arc shape will weaken the sense of boundary, let users feel a wider space, and create the illusion of emptyness to the maximum extent indoors.
Visual effect: The transparent shell of glass gives people a pure and transparent feeling. There will be smoke rising inside the last two layers of glass, giving people a sense of ethereal and isolated, but also visually showing the state of empty.
Overall volume: diameter is about 2.5m
Reason for selection: It can be placed in the general indoor space, which is also in line with the sense of the human body

冥想椅 Meditation Chair

整体造型：圆角矩形为主，凳子腿是圆柱形
选择原因：拉近距离感，提高舒适度
高度：550mm
选择原因：比普通的座椅离地面稍近，冥想打坐的时候更有安全感，更方便。
坐垫：选用一大一小两层坐垫
选择原因：两个坐垫的距离差正好可以缓解盘腿打坐时腿和脚踝的不适感。
Overall shape: mainly rounded rectangle, stool legs are cylindrical
Reason for selection: close sense of distance, improve comfort
Height: 550mm
Reason for selection: It is slightly closer to the ground than an ordinary seat. It is more secure and convenient when meditating.
Cushion: Choose one large and one small two-layer cushion Reason for selection: The difference in distance between the two cushions can just relieve the discomfort of the legs and ankles when sitting cross-legged.

界面设计 interface design

音乐界面 
进入冥想屋后可以自行选择背景音乐。音乐分为四个板块：一是森林的白噪音，二是水流或是雨声的白噪音，三是舒缓的纯音乐，四是语音引导冥想（带领你专注于自己的呼吸、身体、思想）。
Music interface
After entering the meditation room, you can choose your own background music. The music is divided into four sections: one is the white noise of the forest, the other is the white noise of water or rain, the third is soothing pure music, and the fourth is voice-guided meditation (leading you to focus on your breathing, body, and thought) .

Product Instructions

1

首先注意左侧门框的凹槽处，您可以将手机关机后放置其中进行充电.
First pay attention to the groove of the left door frame, you can turn off the phone and place it to charge. 

2

确认手机已处于充电状态中后，并把放置手机的卡座门关闭，嵌于门上的显示屏幕才可自定义装置使用时间
After confirming that the mobile phone is in the charging state, and close the door of the deck where the mobile phone is placed, the display screen embedded on the door can customize the device usage time 

3

设置完以后装置的门会自动打开，待体验者进入之后会按照自定义时间进行锁定。
The door of the device will automatically open after the setting is completed, and will be locked according to the customized time after the experiencer enters.

4

进入空间后可以在门上的显示屏幕，也可自行选择（可以选择无音乐或是音乐歌单内的曲目）
 After entering the space, you can display the screen on the door or choose it yourself (you can choose no music or tracks in the music song list)

5

以上操作完成以后即可坐上冥想椅进行冥想了
After the above operations are completed, you can sit on the meditation chair for meditation.
